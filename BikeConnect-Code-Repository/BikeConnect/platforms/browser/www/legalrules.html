Keeping Safe Biking in Dubai

Here are some tips for you to keep safe in Dubai while biking

About

It is mandatory to wear a helmet when riding a bicycle in Dubai.  It is necessary to carry your ID proof at all the times. Cyclists found riding on roads with speed limits of 60kmph or greater may be fined up to AED 600.  Fine of AED 200 for bike riders who use paths dedicated for pedestrians, while riding in a way that endangers pedestrians can get you fined AED 300. Cyclists are encouraged to use internal roads and dedicated cycle paths. Double seat riding is not allowed unless the bike is designed for two people. Offenders may be fined AED 200. Give right of way to pedestrians. Always park your bicycle in a parking lot with lock. It is advisable to use front and rear lights for night rides. It is advisable to wear reflective clothing.
